About Taniti's Transportation

Taniti's airport makes it easy to get to our wonderful island, and it will soon be even easier. Our ongoing airport expansion project will open up Taniti to even more vistors. Cruise ships are another popular travel method, and bring people weekly.

Once on the island, Taniti offers a variety of options to make getting around the island a breeze. We have public and private busses, as well as car rentals. Bikes are also for rent, and the island is considered very walkable for those who like to travel by foot.

Taniti Transportation FAQ

Q: Where can I rent a car?
A: The local rental agency near the airport offers car rentals.

Q: What hours do the busses run?
A: Public busses operate from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. daily.
