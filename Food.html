About Taniti's Eateries

Taniti features a vibrant dining scene that is sure to please. The island has 10 wonderful restaurants that offer unique culinary experiences. From local to American style, our restaurants are going to dazzle.

In addition to our highly rated restaurants, there are two supermarkets, two grocery stores, and a 24-hour convenience if you are in the mood for island produce, fresh-caught seafood, or local snacks!

Taniti Eateries FAQ
