Welcome to Taniti - Your Island Getaway

Come visit the paradise in the Pacific, where tradition meets modern. Taniti is the ultimate destination for beauty, relaxation, and exploration. From sandy beaches, to lush rainforests, Taniti's beauty will take your breath away.

Taniti's 20,000 locals have a rich culture that is shared with open arms to visitors. Our guests love to learn about the history and culture of the wonderful people of Taniti.

At the heart of Taniti, is Merriton Landing, a growing tourist hub with many new modern amenities. Don't worry, it still has that Taniti charm that the island is famous for.

Taniti FAQ

Q: What is the language that is spoken on the island?
A: Many residents speak English, especially in the tourism areas. Local languages are spoken in rural areas.

Q: What currencies are accepted?
A: Taniti uses the dollar for currency, but yen and euro are accepted at some locations within the island as well. Credit cards are widely accepted, as well.

Q: Are there medical facilities?
A: YES! Taniti has a renowned hospital, as well as easy to access smaller clinics across the island.
