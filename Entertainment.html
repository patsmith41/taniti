About Taniti's Entertainment and Nightlife

Taniti offers a wide range of entertainment, sure to excite all visitors. During the day, visit the beautiful beaches, lush rainforests, and an active volcano! If you are feeling adventurous, try zip-lining through the rainforest. If it is ocean fun you seek, try snorkeling or a chartered fishing tour.

Q: What is the main entertainment area?
A: Merriton Island, on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay, is the entertainment hub of the island.
