About Taniti's Lodging

Taniti prides itself on being a destination for every type of traveler. We have 4-star hotels for the traveler seeking luxury and opulence. We have celebrated bed and breakfasts for those who want to get a taste of the local flavor. And we have hostels for the adventurous backpackers.

Taniti Lodging FAQ

Q: Which area should I stay in?
A: Merriton Island is a popular place to stay due to the modern amenities and closeness to beaches and attractions. Taniti City is popular for its rich culture. The rainforest offers the ultimate peaceful escape.

Q: Is it expensive?
A: NO! Taniti is a very budget friendly destination.
